SpeakerSphere Implementation Plan (Prompt-Based)
Phase 0: Foundation & Tier Visibility

Goal: Establish visible differentiation (Basic | Pro | Premier) within the existing SpeakerSphere site.
Prompts you’ll use for this phase:

“Write the descriptive text for each tier’s public badge so that visitors immediately know what each level offers.”

“Create the exact wording for the homepage section that explains how speakers are featured or rotated by tier.”

“Draft a concise line that appears on every speaker profile describing what tier they are and what that includes.”

“Give me short, value-based blurbs for Basic | Pro | Premier that I can place under a ‘Compare Plans’ header.”

Key deliverables (all written):

Badge wording, hover tooltips, and tier comparison text.

Homepage tagline copy for Premier-featured speakers.

Directory label language (e.g., ‘Featured Speaker’, ‘Premier Spotlight’).

Phase 1: Subscription & Value Communication

Goal: Present clear, conversion-focused language around upgrading tiers and explaining benefits.
Prompts you’ll use:

“Write a paragraph describing the Pro plan in a persuasive but professional tone for the pricing page.”

“Give me microcopy for the upgrade buttons (action-driven, short).”

“Create email wording for post-signup messages welcoming new subscribers to each tier.”

“Draft the confirmation text users see after purchasing or downgrading.”

Deliverables:

Pricing page copy, CTA language, onboarding email text, and in-app success messages.

Phase 2: The Vault and Exclusive Content

Goal: Establish a branded identity and experience for the “Vault” feature available to Premier speakers.
Prompts you’ll use:

“Write compelling text introducing ‘The Vault’ as a secure, premium content library.”

“Create the short instruction text for planners explaining how to access password-protected materials.”

“Provide tone-consistent warning/error messages for incorrect passwords or expired links.”

“Write professional confirmation text when a planner successfully unlocks Vault content.”

Deliverables:

Vault description copy, access instructions, and error/success language.

Phase 3: Calendar & Booking Flow

Goal: Create the human-facing text for availability, booking, and communication between planners and speakers.
Prompts you’ll use:

“Write friendly but professional text for the ‘Check Availability’ button and calendar modal.”

“Give me the short placeholder wording for the booking request form (name, organization, message).”

“Draft the thank-you confirmation that appears after a booking request is submitted.”

“Write the automated email response to a planner confirming their request was received.”

Deliverables:

Booking modal copy, form field guidance, confirmation text, and email responses.

Phase 4: Premier Experience & Analytics

Goal: Communicate exclusivity and insight for Premier speakers and their analytics dashboard.
Prompts you’ll use:

“Write dashboard section headings that sound professional yet motivating (‘Your Reach This Month’, ‘Top Inquiries’, etc.).”

“Draft a short paragraph explaining how to interpret analytics metrics like views, clicks, and inquiries.”

“Create celebratory or progress-based language for milestones (e.g., ‘You’ve reached 500 profile views!’).”

“Give me concise tooltip wording that explains each analytics metric.”

Deliverables:

Dashboard text set (titles, tooltips, explanations, and milestone language).

Phase 5: Community & Brand Consistency

Goal: Build a unified SpeakerSphere voice across the site, emails, and promotional material.
Prompts you’ll use:

“Write the SpeakerSphere mission statement in one sentence for the footer.”

“Create the tagline for marketing emails announcing new featured speakers.”

“Draft brand-voice guidelines: tone, vocabulary, emotional energy, and do’s/don’ts.”

“Write a one-paragraph brand story that connects SpeakerSphere to its purpose of elevating speakers.”

Deliverables:

Unified voice guide, marketing tagline, mission statement, and story paragraph.
